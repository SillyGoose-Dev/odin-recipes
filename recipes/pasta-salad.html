Pasta Salad Recipe

This healthy pasta salad recipe is a summer favorite. It is perfect if you have an abundant garden than this is the perfect recipe to use some of those extra veggies! This is a versatile side dish that is sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rotini pasta
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes halved
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 3.8 ounce can sliced black olives
  • 4 ounces mozzarella cheese cubed
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan
  • 1 cup italian dressing
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions for al dente. Drain, rinse with cool water, then drain well and transfer to a large bowl.
  2. Set the pasta aside to cool while you prepare the remaining ingredients, stirring occasionally.
  3. While the pasta cools, chop the vegetables and other ingredients.
  4. Add the bell peppers, tomatoes, red onion, olives, cubed mozzarella, Parmesan and parsley to the bowl with the cooled pasta. Stir in the dressing until well combined. Taste and season with extra salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving. Stir pasta salad again right before serving. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed, serve.
