Homemade Corn Dogs

'

Corn dogs are an American summer staple! Let me show you how to make them easily with your airfryer at home!

Ingredients

      4 hot dogs
      1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
      1/4 cup all purpose flour
      2 tsp granulated sugar
      1 tsp kosher salt
      1 tsp baking powder
      1/2 cup buttermilk
      cooking spray

Steps

  1. Turn on your air fryer to 375F
  2. Place the cornmeal, eggs, buttermilk, sugar, salt, and baking powder in a tall glass and mix until smooth.
  3. Push the skewer into the end of the hotdog 3/4 of the way.
  4. Roll the skewered hot dog in flour until evenly coated.
  5. Hold the skewed and dip the hot dog into the glass of batter.
  6. Add the battered corn dogs to the preheated air fryer basket. Make sure you leave plenty of room between them.
  7. Cover the corn dogs with a light layers of cooking spray to help them crisp.
  8. Air fry for 8 minutes making sure to flip half way through.
